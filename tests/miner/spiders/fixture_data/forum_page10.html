So, before I really hit the wall not knowing I had Lyme - one of my major symptoms were anger, and paranoia. I can successfully say that I no longer have either at all. I went to the Pfieffer Treatment Center because I didn't know it was Lyme, Bartonella and Babesia at play. They did discover I was deficient in zinc which can cause extreme paranoid symptoms. So I took 100mg zinc piconolate (per the doctors advice) for a month and saw a huge turnaround in the paranoia. Pretty much gone. The anger however did not go so quickly. In fact I thought I might be bipolar. Long story short - treating Bartonella for 8 months got rid of the anger/anxiety issues but the paranoia was kicked with the zinc. I continued to supplement zinc for a year or so and now I don't take it at all. Our bodies start having all sorts of deficiencys because they are fighting so hard and zinc deficiency is a known side effect of Lyme (Klinghardt KPU). Anyways, I wish you the best.

I will add that every time I took liposomal glutathione (which detoxes the liver) I felt very angry and irritable within 30 minutes. I believe in chinese medicine when someone gets really angry (like with Lyme) they relate it to the liver i.e. 'angry liver'. It may be a good idea to take some Milk Thistle and begin gently detox the liver to release the toxins that could be irritating it.

Thats my two cents of what worked for me. I know everyone is different. I wish you the very best and I am sorry you are going through it right now.
